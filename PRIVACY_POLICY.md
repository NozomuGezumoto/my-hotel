# Privacy Policy for My Hotel

**Last updated:** February 24, 2026

## Introduction

My Hotel ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application that helps you discover and track luxury hotels around the world. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the app.

## Information We Collect

### Information Stored Locally on Your Device

- **Visit status**: Your "visited" and "want to go" markers for hotels
- **Photos**: Hotel photos you add from your photo library (stored locally only)
- **Notes and ratings**: Memos and satisfaction ratings you write for hotels

All of the above data is stored solely on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

### Permissions We Request

- **Photo Library / Camera Roll**: Used only when you choose to add photos to a hotel. We do not access your photos without your explicit action.

## Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:
- Personal identification (name, email, phone)
- Payment information
- Location history beyond what is needed for map display
- Any data for advertising or analytics

## Third-Party Services

- **Google Maps**: Used to display hotel locations. Google's privacy policy applies to map usage: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Amadeus**: Hotel data (names, locations, prices) is sourced from Amadeus. We do not share your personal data with Amadeus.

## Data Retention

Data you create (photos, notes, ratings) remains on your device until you:
- Delete the app
- Clear app data in your device settings

## Children's Privacy

My Hotel is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects when changes were last made.
