Privacy Policy for My Hotel

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Introduction

My Hotel ("we," "our," or "the app") is a mobile application designed to help users discover, explore, and keep personal records of luxury hotels around the world. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how that information is used, and what choices you have when using the app.

By using My Hotel, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect
1. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

My Hotel allows you to create and manage personal hotel records. The following information may be stored locally on your device:

Visit status
Such as whether you marked a hotel as "visited" or "want to go."

Photos you choose to add
Hotel-related photos that you manually select from your photo library.

Notes and ratings
Personal memos, comments, and satisfaction ratings you create for hotels.

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Permissions We Request
Photo Library Access

My Hotel may request access to your photo library only when you choose to attach a photo to one of your personal hotel records.

For example, you may select a photo of a hotel you visited and attach it to your saved record along with your own notes or rating.

We do not access your photo library unless you explicitly choose to add a photo.

How We Use Information

The information handled by the app is used only to support app features requested by you, including:

letting you keep track of hotels you visited or want to visit

allowing you to attach your own photos to hotel records

saving your personal notes and ratings

displaying hotel information and map-based exploration within the app

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or analytics.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store the following personal data on our servers:

Your name

Your email address

Your phone number

Payment or billing information

Account credentials

Your personal contact list

Your personal photo library content unless you explicitly select a photo for local use in the app

Your location history

Any data for advertising or behavioral tracking

Any analytics data linked to you as an identifiable user

Location Information

My Hotel may display hotel locations on a map as part of the app experience.

The app does not store your location history on our servers.
If map-related services use location functionality on your device, that use is handled according to your device settings and the policies of the relevant platform or map provider.

Third-Party Services

My Hotel may rely on third-party services to provide certain app functions.

Google Maps

We use Google Maps to display hotel locations and related map content inside the app.
Use of Google Maps is subject to Google's own privacy policy and terms.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Amadeus

Hotel information, such as hotel names, locations, and pricing-related reference data, may be sourced from Amadeus.
We do not share your personal notes, ratings, or locally stored photos with Amadeus.

Data Storage and Retention

The data you create in My Hotel, such as visit status, notes, ratings, and selected photos, remains on your device unless you remove it.

This data may be removed when you:

delete the relevant content inside the app

delete the app from your device

clear app data through your device settings, where applicable

Because the data is stored locally, we generally cannot access, recover, or delete it on your behalf.

Data Security

We are committed to minimizing data collection and limiting data handling to what is necessary for the app's functionality.

Because most user-created content in My Hotel is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device, including device passcodes, backups, and access control.

Children's Privacy

My Hotel is not directed to children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will review the situation appropriately.

International Users

My Hotel may be used by users in different countries. However, the app is designed to minimize server-side collection of personal data. Most user-created data remains stored locally on the user's device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app, legal requirements, or operational needs.

When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any update indicates acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
